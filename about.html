About Me

As an undergraduate of BSc Honours Computer Science at NSBM Green University, I am a skilled backend developer with expertise in programming languages such as Go, Java, TypeScript, and Python. I have hands-on experience with tools like Node, Prisma, and frameworks like Go Fiber, Next.js, and Express. I am also well-versed in AWS, Google Cloud, and Docker, which I utilize to build robust and scalable backend systems. Additionally, I have strong proficiency in databases, including MySQL, PostgreSQL, Firebase, MongoDB, and Redis. As a co-founder of Szeeta, a dynamic startup, I am passionate about creating innovative solutions that drive meaningful impact. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of software development principles, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality code and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in software development.
